\cvsection{Interests}
\begin{cventries}

  \cvtextentry{
    My main research interest lies in the mathematical foundations of
    static program analysis, particularly the theory and application
    of abstract interpretation. I enjoy designing and implementing
    abstract domains that capture complex program behaviors —
    recently, this has involved developing a domain for R vectors
    that handles indexing, recycling, and missing values. Beyond this
    core focus, I am broadly drawn to theoretical computer science,
    formal methods, and mathematical logic. I also enjoy reasoning
    about algorithms and working on language processing tools such as
    parsers.
  }

\end{cventries}
